Built With Small Businesses In Mind

Small businesses live and die by word-of-mouth referrals. Tethyr automates the way companies control their word-of-mouth.

Unfortunately, your client's aren't always talking about you. Social media post get buried quickly, reviews are impersonal and anonymized, and search engines favor the big companies.
